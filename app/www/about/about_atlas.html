The Atlas of City Transportation is an online data dashboard for indicators of sustainable and inclusive urban passenger mobility. It includes measurements of ZZ indicators for over XXX metropolitan areas and YYYY jurisdictions or districts worldwide.

Our indicators are designed to measure what matters for transportation in cities. In general, we measure the number of people who live near a certain kind of infrastructure, rather than the amount of that infrastructure. For example, 100km of bicycle lanes in a low-density semi-rural area might be less useful than 10km of bicycle lanes in the city center.
